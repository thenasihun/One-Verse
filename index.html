Guide: How to Use One Verse

  • 1

    Pick Your Voice

    Go to Settings and select from 20 world-class reciters and your preferred translation language.

  • 2

    Navigate with Ease

    On the home screen, tap the Surah or Ayah name to open a beautiful selection grid. Find any verse in seconds.

  • 3

    Open the Player

    Tap the 'Playlist' icon or 'Open in Player' to start your lag-free journey. Use the Toggle icon to switch between Arabic and Translation views.

  • 4

    Download & Create

    Long press any verse in the playlist to select and download. Use the audio in your local 'Music/OneVerse' folder for content creation.
