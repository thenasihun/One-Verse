Privacy Policy for One Verse

Last Updated: December 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to One Verse. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information Collection and Use

  • Personal Data: One Verse does not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your app usage habits or store any data on external servers. All settings like font size, theme, and selected reciter are stored locally on your device.

3. Permissions

To provide our core features, the app requests the following permissions:

  • Storage / Media / Audio Access: Used only to download Quranic audio files into your public Music folder. The app does not access personal photos or private files.
  • Internet Access: Required to stream audio recitations and fetch Quranic text and translations from third-party APIs.

4. Third-Party Services

One Verse uses the AlQuran.cloud API to fetch Quranic text and audio links. While we do not collect data, third-party services may process your IP address to fulfill requests. We encourage users to review their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being leaked from our servers. Any downloaded content remains fully under your control on your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

One Verse is safe for all ages and does not contain age-restricted content. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
